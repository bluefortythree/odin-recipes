BURGER RECIPE

Description

This burger can be catered towards your preferences and all the ingredients are interchangeable/optional.

INGREDIENTS

  • buns
  • lettuce
  • tomatoes
  • bacon
  • onions
  • all natural angus beef

STEPS

  1. format patty into ball shape and press
  2. cook until rawness desire
  3. cut up vegetables, put on buns
  4. put patty on buns
  5. serve and enjoy
